Health Information

When to Seek Emergency Care +
Seek immediate medical attention for:
- Chest pain or pressure
- Difficulty breathing
- Sudden severe headache
- Uncontrolled bleeding
- Signs of stroke (face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty)
- Severe allergic reactions
- Loss of consciousness
Understanding BMI +
BMI Categories:
- Underweight: Below 18.5
- Normal: 18.5 - 24.9
- Overweight: 25.0 - 29.9
- Obese: 30.0 and above

BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnostic. Consult your healthcare provider for a complete health assessment.
Normal Vital Signs +
Adult normal ranges:
- Heart Rate: 60-100 bpm
- Blood Pressure: 90/60 - 120/80 mmHg
- Respiratory Rate: 12-20 breaths/min
- Temperature: 97.8-99.1°F (36.5-37.3°C)
- Oxygen Saturation: 95-100%
First Aid Basics +
Burns: Cool under running water for 10+ min. Cover loosely.
Cuts: Apply pressure with clean cloth. Elevate the wound.
Choking: 5 back blows, then 5 abdominal thrusts (Heimlich).
CPR: 30 chest compressions, 2 breaths. Push hard and fast.
